# Welcome to Auditvia

We're excited to introduce **Auditvia**, a comprehensive accessibility monitoring platform designed to help organizations maintain and improve their website accessibility compliance.

### Key Statistics

- **71%** of users with disabilities will leave a website that isn't accessible

- **$6.2 billion** in legal settlements related to web accessibility in 2023

## What is Auditvia?

Auditvia is an automated accessibility monitoring platform that helps you:

- **Monitor Continuously**: Set up automated daily scans for your websites
- **Track Progress**: View detailed accessibility scores and trend analysis
- **Fix Issues**: Get actionable insights with detailed violation reports
- **Stay Compliant**: Ensure ongoing WCAG 2.1 AA compliance
